Orthodontic Dentistry Facts

1) Orthodontics is a dentistry specialty that addresses the diagnosis, prevention, and correction of mal-positioned teeth and jaws, and misaligned bite patterns.

2) A patient with TMJ issues may exprience jaw pain and headaches. Braces are great for correcting issues such as these. Through aligning your mouth and teeth, you can prevent that pain from appearing. Itâ€™s also a great way to prevent these issues from developing in the first place.

3) Braces were invented in 1819 using a set of wire crib braces. Today there are multiple options to choose from!

-The most traditional type of braces you can have are metal braces. This type has metal brackets that are attached to the teeth and tightened with a wire.
-For a more concealed look, you can opt for ceramic braces. Functionally, they work in the same fashion as traditional metal braces. Their difference comes from their look.
-Lingual braces are another option that is virtually invisible. Instead of being attached to the front of the teeth like metal and ceramic braces, this type goes on the back of the teeth.
-The last type is Invisalign which is removable, unlike the other types. They are clear trays that you put on your teeth to align them.
