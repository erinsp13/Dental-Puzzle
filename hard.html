Oral Surgery Facts

1) Restorative dentistry is the study, diagnosis and integrated management of diseases of the teeth and their supporting structures and the rehabilitation of the dentition to functional and aesthetic requirements of the individual

2) To put it simply, restorative dentistry involves any dental process which focuses on repairing or restoring damaged oral structures. This can include procedures such as fillings, bridges, and crowns among many other services

3) Restorative dentistry plays an integral role in boosting your overall oral health. Restoring cracked or decayed teeth prevents further infection. Dental bridges, implants, and dentures are used to strengthen your jawbone ]
